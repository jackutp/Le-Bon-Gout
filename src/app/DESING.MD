# 🍣 LE BON GOUT – Premium Restaurant Design System

## 1. Design Philosophy

This design system combines principles from Airbnb (experience-driven), Apple (minimalist premium), and Linear (dark modern elegance).

**Core Principles:**

* Experience-first (Airbnb)
* Minimalism & clarity (Apple)
* Sophisticated dark UI (Linear)

---

## 2. Visual Identity

### Color Palette

* Primary Background: #0B0B0C (deep black)
* Secondary Background: #121214
* Accent Gold: #C6A96B
* Warm Light: #F5E6C8
* Text Primary: #FFFFFF
* Text Secondary: #A1A1AA

### Usage

* Use dark backgrounds to evoke exclusivity
* Apply gold only for highlights (buttons, accents)
* Avoid excessive color variety

---

## 3. Typography

### Headings (Apple-inspired)

* Font: Playfair Display / Serif
* Style: Elegant, large spacing

### Body (Airbnb-inspired)

* Font: Inter / Sans-serif
* Style: Clean, readable

### Rules

* Large hero titles
* Minimal text blocks
* Strong hierarchy

---

## 4. Layout System

### Structure

* Grid-based layout (12 columns)
* Generous whitespace
* Section separation using contrast

### Spacing

* Large padding (80px+ sections)
* Consistent margins

---

## 5. Components

### Navbar

* Transparent → solid on scroll
* Minimal items: Menu, Experience, Reservations

### Hero Section

* Full-screen image/video
* Overlay gradient (dark + warm tone)
* Large title + short subtitle

### Menu Section

* Card-based layout
* High-quality food photography
* Minimal descriptions

### Buttons

* Primary: Gold background, dark text
* Secondary: Outline gold
* Rounded edges (8–12px)

---

## 6. Imagery & Media

* Use high-resolution food photography
* Warm lighting
* Focus on texture and detail
* Avoid stock-looking images

---

## 7. Interaction & UX

### Animations (Linear-inspired)

* Subtle fade-ins
* Smooth transitions (200–300ms)
* Hover effects: scale (1.02–1.05)

### Behavior

* Fast loading
* Smooth scrolling
* Responsive design mandatory

---

## 8. Tone & Content

* Short, refined copy
* Emotional but not exaggerated
* Focus on experience and exclusivity

Example:

> "A culinary journey crafted with precision and passion."

---

## 9. Do & Don’t

### Do

✔ Use whitespace generously
✔ Focus on visuals
✔ Keep UI minimal
✔ Maintain consistency

### Don’t

✘ Overcrowd screens
✘ Use too many colors
✘ Add unnecessary text
✘ Use generic UI components

---

## 10. Implementation Notes

* Prefer dark mode by default
* Optimize images (WebP/AVIF)
* Use lazy loading
* Ensure accessibility (contrast, font size)

---

## 11. Inspiration Summary

* Airbnb → Experience & storytelling
* Apple → Minimalism & premium feel
* Linear → Dark modern UI & motion

---

**Goal:** Deliver a luxury digital experience that reflects a high-end restaurant brand.
